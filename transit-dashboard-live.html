⚡ One-time setup for live data

Open Terminal, navigate to the folder with this file, and run:

python3 -m http.server 8080

Then open http://localhost:8080/transit-dashboard-live.html in Chrome.

This takes 10 seconds and makes live DVB data work permanently.
